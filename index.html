Healthy Chicken Stir-Fry

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbs Olive Oil
  • 1 Cup Broccoli Florets
  • 1 Cup Sliced Bell Peppers
  • 4 Oz Diced Chicken to 3/4 Inch Cubes
  • 1 Cup Brown Rice
  • 2 Whole Eggs

    • Seasonings

  • 1 Tbs Bragg's Liquid Aminos
  • Pinch of Pink Himalayan Salt
  • Pinch of Fresh Ground Black Pepper

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a wok until smoking.
  2. Add diced chicken and cook until each cube reaches an internal temperature of about 160 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. Add in brown rice.
  4. Scramble the two eggs and stir-fry in with brown rice and chicken.
  5. Add in all the vegetables.
  6. Add in all the seasonings to taste.
